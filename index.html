The Story

About Fiona

Born in Metz, France and raised in Limerick, Ireland, Fiona brings a unique blend of engineering precision and athletic talent to professional cycling.

After graduating with a distinction in Biomedical Engineering from NUI Galway and working in cutting-edge implant design at Loci Orthopaedics, Fiona made the bold transition to professional cycling in 2020 — joining Greenmount Cycling Club and rapidly rising through the ranks.

In just four years, she went from club rider to double National Champion, claiming both the Irish Road Race and Time Trial titles in 2024. In 2023, she became the first Irish woman to finish a Grand Tour at La Vuelta Femenina — a landmark moment for Irish cycling.

Fiona's analytical background gives her a tactical edge on the road, while her determination and natural ability make her a standout rider in the peloton. She speaks English, French, and Spanish fluently — an asset in the international world of professional cycling.
